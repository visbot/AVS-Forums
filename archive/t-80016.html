By the way Rovastar, you don't need to dock AVS to configure it... simply double-click the AVS display to open the preset editor/options.

The reason the 'config' button doesn't work is because AVS can only be configured while it is running (logically). When you press the config button, Winamp loads the plug-in DLL but doesn't give sound data to it... (you're only 'configuring' it).
